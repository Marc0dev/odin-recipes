Pasta al Pesto Recipe

Pasta al pesto is an Italian dish consisting of cooked pasta tossed in a sauce made with fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. It's a flavorful and aromatic dish that can be enjoyed as is or paired with proteins like chicken or shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (such as spaghetti or Penne)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Garlic cloves
  • Pine nuts
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the pesto sauce. In a food processor or blender, combine the fresh basil leaves, garlic cloves, pine nuts, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste.
  4. Gradually drizzle in the olive oil while continuing to blend until the sauce reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. You may need to scrape down the sides of the processor or blender as needed.
  5. Season the pesto sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the flavors as desired.
  6. Drain the cooked pasta, reserving a small amount of the cooking water.
  7. In a large bowl, toss the drained pasta with the prepared pesto sauce. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce and help it coat the pasta evenly.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  9. Serve the pasta al pesto immediately, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil leaves, and any desired optional ingredients.
  10. Enjoy the delicious flavors of pasta al pesto!

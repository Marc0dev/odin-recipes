Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with alternating layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese. The pasta sheets are typically pre-cooked, and the meat sauce can be made with ground beef, sausage, or a combination of both, cooked with onions, garlic, tomatoes, and herbs. Each layer is topped with a generous amount of cheese, usually mozzarella and Parmesan. The entire dish is baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a delicious and hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature according to the lasagna sheets' packaging instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and continue cooking until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to the skillet. Season with Italian herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom.
  5. Place a layer of lasagna sheets on top of the sauce, covering it completely.
  6. Spoon a generous amount of the meat sauce over the lasagna sheets, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers, alternating between lasagna sheets, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese until you've used up all the ingredients. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, ensuring it's tightly sealed to trap the steam.
  9. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
  12. Slice and serve the lasagna while it's still hot and enjoy!
